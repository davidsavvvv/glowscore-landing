Surface container — #111 fill, hairline border, 2xl radius.

```jsx
<Card>Résumé de l'analyse…</Card>
<Card glass>Floating panel over a glow blob</Card>
<Card accent>Highlighted pricing tier</Card>
<Card glow padding={24}>Score panel with ambient gold glow</Card>
```

Flags: `glass` (translucent + blur), `glow` (gold ambient shadow), `accent` (gold border). Depth comes from the gold glow, not grey drop-shadows.
